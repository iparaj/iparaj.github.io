This is a minor course for the third year B.Tech students of IIT Guwahati. Signal processing being an essential component in any field of engineering, the aim of this course is to introduce 
the concepts of signals and systems to the students who are not from the Electronics and Electrical Department of the institute. The syllabus for the course can be found here: [[Link]](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M_Syllabus.pdf)

Modules
=======

* Module 1:  Classification of signals and systems
	* [Types of Signals](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module1a-Types_of_signals.pdf) 
	* [Operations on Signals](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module1b-Operations_on_signals.pdf) 
	* [Systems and properties of systems](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module1c-Systems_and_their_properties.pdf) 
* Module 2:  LTI Systems, z-Transform and Laplace Transform
	* [LTI Systems](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-2a-LTI_Systems.pdf) 
	* [Properties of LTI Systems](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-2b-Properties_LTI_Systems.pdf) 
	* [Difference equations and poles](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-2c-Difference_equations_poles.pdf)
	* [Differential equations and poles](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-2d-Differential_equations_poles.pdf)
* Module 3: [z-Transform](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-3-z-Transform.pdf) 
* Module 4: [Laplace Transform](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-4-Laplace-Transform.pdf)
* Module 5: Fourier series and Fourier Transform
	* [Continuous Time Fourier Series (CTFS)](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-5a CT_Fourier_Series.pdf) 
	* [Convergence of CTFS](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-5b Convergence_CT_Fourier_Series.pdf) 
	* [Properties of CTFS](http://chayanbhawal.github.io/files/EE322M/Module-5c Properties_of_CT_Fourier_Series.pdf)
